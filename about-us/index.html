Get data and key metrics in real-time

CoviDefense monitors potential symptoms and transmitters of COVID-19, such as coughing and touching your face.

Machine Learning

CoviDefense uses human pose estimation to detect:

  • Instances where someone touches their face
  • When an individual coughs

Dashboard

CoviDefense tallies instances where an individual coughs and/or touches their face, and relays it to a dashboard, where government officials can use the data to predict potential COVID-19 outbreaks.
